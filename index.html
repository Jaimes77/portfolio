About Me

For the past two years, I have been the full-time caretaker to my 92 year old Grandmother who suffers from Alzheimer's Disease. Prior to caring for my Gram, as I like to call her, I worked in the food and beverage industry mainly as a bartender/manager. When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I saw the impact the pandemic had and continues to have on restaurants and bars and decided I needed to add to my skill set.

So...I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy in their Web Development Bootcamp. While I am new to this world of coding, I am very much enjoying my time learning this new skill set.

And when I'm not caring for Gram or learning Javascript, I enjoy video games; Mass Effect and Dragon's Age being two of my all time favorites. But my most favorite thing to do is ballroom dance!
